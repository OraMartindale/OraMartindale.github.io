Meet
The Martindales

Enid & Ora met at their first real jobs. Enid asked Ora out and our first date was the movie Blade. A little more than a year later we were no longer co-workers but we were Wife & Husband. A little more than a year after that we found out we were going to be Mom & Dad. Now we have three kids.

Soon after our first child was born we began family vacations. We started with Yellowstone and the Grand Tetons and most recently visited Revolutionary War battlefields and NYC. We've taken our kids to 26 states and counting.
